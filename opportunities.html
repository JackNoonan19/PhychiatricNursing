Undertaking this course allows you to enjoy the student life that awaits you. Events throughout the year that allow you to meet new people, make new friends and have fun during your study.

Our location also gives you the opportunity to explore Waterford city. During the day, in relative distance are the local mountains, a fantastic spot to hike and spend a day with friends. Tramore beach being only a bus ride away provides a great location to relax during your breaks to get away.
The night life in Waterford also allows you to relax and have fun more consistently with the wide variety of pubs to explore and experience.
