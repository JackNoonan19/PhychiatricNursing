Psychiatric nursing allows you to under take responsibilities regarding patients mental health

This course will allow you to grow and learn ideas and practices to help understand and care for patients suffering from mental illnesses and traumas.

Starting in the first semester, you will be undertaking clinical placements for experience and knowledge

There are over 80 weeks of these clinical placements in this course to gain experience in, along with 36 continuous weeks of internship placement in the four year in places such as Waterford, Wexford, Kilkenny, Carlow and South Tipperary. This course runs for a total of 4 acedmic years. This course deviates from other medical based coursed as it focuses much more on mental and psychological issues patients face.
